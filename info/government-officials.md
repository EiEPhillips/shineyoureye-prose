[National Assembly](http://www.nassnig.org "National Assembly")

------------------

The National Assembly is Nigeriaâ€™s bicameral legislature and the highest elective law-making body of the country. It consists of the 109-member [**Senate**](http://www.nassnig.org/nass/index.php "Senate") and the 360-member [**House of Representatives**](http://www.nassnig.org/nass2/index.php "House of Representatives"). The term of the National Assembly is 4-years from the date of its first sitting after the general elections. The current 9th National Assembly (2019-2023) was inaugurated on June 11, 2019.
